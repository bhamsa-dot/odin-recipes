Gulab Jamun

Description

This is a delicious Gulab Jamun recipe that everyone will love! make sure to follow the instructions carefully, and enjoy your meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
  2. Add ghee and mix well until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Gradually add milk and knead the mixture into a smooth dough.
  4. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls.
  5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Fry the balls in batches until they turn golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. In a separate pan, combine sugar and water to make a syrup. Bring it to a boil and add cardamom powder.
  8. take it out on plate
