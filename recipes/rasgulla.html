Rasgulla

Description

This is a delicious rasgulla recipe that everyone will love! make sure to follow the instructions carefully, and enjoy your meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
  • Water for boiling

steps

  1. Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan.
  2. Add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle the milk.
  3. Strain the curdled milk using a muslin cloth to separate the whey.
  4. Knead the chenna (curdled milk) until smooth and soft.
  5. Divide the chenna into small balls and shape them into smooth round shapes.
  6. In a large pan, boil water and add sugar to make a syrup.
  7. Add the chenna balls to the boiling syrup and cook for 15-20 minutes until they double in size.
  8. Add cardamom powder for flavor and let them cool before serving.
